------------------------------------------------------------
ITU standard E.123 (phone number syntax)
------------------------------------------------------------


>>> NATIONAL PHONE NUMBER SYNTAX:
(123) 456 7890


>>> INTERNATIONAL PHONE NUMBER SYNTAX:
+1 123 456 7890


------------------------------------------------------------

>>> BREAKDOWN:

  Phone numbers should...
    ✔USE✔ Space delimitation 
    ✔USE✔ parenthesis to enclose optional parts of a national phone number
    ╳NOT╳ contain any hyphen (-) character(s)
    ╳NOT╳ contain any En Dash (–) character(s)
    ╳NOT╳ contain any Em Dash (—) character(s)
