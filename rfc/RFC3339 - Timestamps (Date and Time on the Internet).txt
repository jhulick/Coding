RFC3339 - Timestamps (Date and Time on the Internet)

------------------------------------------------------------

PowerShell, Input:  Get-Date "2019-11-23T17:00:50+00:00";
PowerShell, Output:  Get-Date -UFormat "%Y%m%d%H%M%S%Z";
